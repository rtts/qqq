The community quest for the ultimate collection of quiz questions

Quality Quiz Questions is a community of quizzers who develop quiz questions about a variety of topics. We strive for the ultimate quiz question: a question that is formulated creatively but accurate, brings out knowledge you did not know you possessed, and makes you curious to find out more about the topic.

The result for the visitors of this site is a constantly growing database of quality quiz questions that are available for free under the terms of the Creative Commons BY-SA license. You can browse through ready-to-use collections of questions, or make a selection yourself and download it in various file formats.

The result for our community members is a discussion forum with a common goal and many interesting topics for debate. Want to join? Become a member! As a member you can place posts and reactions, rate the quality and difficulty of questions, submit new questions and propose improvements for existing questions.

We are always looking for user's feedback. Do you have requests for certain functionalities, ideas, or complaints? Mail all you feedback to feedback@qualityquizquestions.org
